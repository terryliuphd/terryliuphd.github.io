Introduction

Summary

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Electronics Engineering at National Chiao Tung University, working with Prof. Juinn-Dar Huang. My PhD dissertation topic is design automation for microfluidic biochips, within the broad area of electronic design automation (EDA). I model the behavior of fluids on biochips, design the chip structure, and develop algorithms to solve the design problems. I am currently leading a research team consisting of Master and Undergrad students under the instruction of Prof. Huang, and have published several papers of biochip design automation at top-tier journal/conferences. My research interests include high-level synthesis, design automation for VLSI, machine learning, bio-electronics, and algorithm design for biochips.
